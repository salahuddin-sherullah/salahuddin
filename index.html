Music


I started taking interest in music back when I was in school. I was highly influenced by various hip hop artists, particularly Eminem, and started writing my own lyrics. Soon I also started mixing my own tracks and experimenting with different sounds. Ever since, I have continued to compose music using various pluggins, instruments, and sample packs. I found electronic music to be very powerful and was highly moved by how the mixing of different sounds can move the soul and the body. My tracks are an attempt to express my feelings and personality, just like my writings.

Data


I have been a data enthusiast for as far as I can remember. Although, I believe we have to be very responsible when using data, I think it is also essential that we base our decisions on ethically collected data that does not compromise someone's privacy. This is true especially when we consider social interventions that directly impact the livelihoods of the most susceptible communities and individuals. There is a big concern regarding data usage to target vulnerable factions of the society. Unfortunately, Pakistan has lax data regulations which makes it relatively easy to steal someone's data for elicit purposes. A recent rise in predatory lending apps is a case in point.

This section of the site is dedicated to my projects related to data science. Here you can explore my portfolio and look up the various resources that I use in my data science journey. You can access my Jupyter notebooks and data visualization projects on my github.

A quantum leap

Change is a natural part of being human. As life goes on, we constantly adapt to new experiences, challenges, and chances. From birth to different phases of life, we change, letting go of old ideas, learning new things, and embracing what's different. This could be due to personal growth, shifts in society, or just the passage of time. Change shapes who we are and helps us move forward. These shifts in our lives are like threads woven into a complex pattern. Each change leaves a lasting mark. This section is about capturing these important changes in my life – looking back at events that have influenced how I see the world.
