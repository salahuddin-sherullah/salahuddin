I'm an Islamabad-based data analyst, monitoring and evaluation specialist, and policy analyst dedicated to building and evangelizing data-driven policies and interventions aimed at improving the livelihoods of vulnerable populations ■

Climate Alpha

Data Analyst • Oct 2022 — Present

Joining Climate Alpha was like a dream come true. I aimed to set myself on a trajectory that would enable me to both utilize and develop my data skills. Climate Alpha provided me the opportunity to do both. The organization has proven to be the ideal platform for me to translate my aspirations into reality, offering an environment where my data-oriented ambitions could truly flourish.

At Climate Alpha, I am assisting a team of researchers and data scientists in identifying and analyzing datasets and features to drive future-proof real estate strategies under multiple climate scenarios. In the course of a relatively brief duration, I've undergone an incredible learning curve, acquiring an extensive array of new skills, particularly through my involvement with physical climate risk data.

Karandaaz Pakistan

Program Analyst, Digital Finance • March 2020 — Oct 2022

Right after graduating from Boston University, I landed a job at Karandaaz Pakistan. Over the course of two and a half years, I assisted the team in implementing some of the key projects aimed at improving financial inclusion in Pakistan. My main role was to monitor the implementation of BMGF’s Financial Services for the Poor (FSP) program by ensuring the delivery of program’s strategic objectives and outcome indicators.

At Karandaaz Pakistan, I got to play a key role in advancing the agenda of financial inclusion. However, I was wriggling for a research and data analytics role. This is where Climate Alpha emerged as my imminent destination.

It was at BU that I came to the realization that you can never be overeducated ■

Boston University

Masters in Global Policy • Jan 2018 — Dec 2019

I studied global policy at Boston University as a Fulbright Scholar. The Masters program at BU provided me with numerous opportunities to expand my knowledge and challenge my worldview. I had the opportunity to learn from distinguished scholars, including Adil Najam, whose teachings sparked within me a profound curiosity about climate-related policies. This is also where I first practically encountered the world of data science.

The Aman Foundation

Management Trainee, Monitoring and Evaluation • Jan 2018 — Dec 2019

I joined Aman Foundation as part of the first cohort of Management Trainees called the Aman Leaders. I worked with multiple departments on a rotational basis and helped design, implement, monitor, and evaluate various donor sponsored projects.

At IBA, I got to study and compete with some of the best in the country ■

Institute of Business Administration

Bachelors in Business Administration • Jan 2018 — Dec 2019

IBA presented unprecedented scale and diversity of opportunity. My four years of phenomenal professors across the disciplines pushed me to expand, challenge, and adjust my worldview.

My experience living and studying with exceptional students from across Pakistan showed me the power of empathetic leadership and expanded my comfort zone. My engagement in IBA's music and arts initiatives exercised my creative muscle, sparked my obsession with music, and strengthened my tolerance to adversity. IBA showed me what I love to do, gifted me with a love for learning, and surrounded me with a wealth of inspiring peers.

AKHSS Gilgit was the most profound experience of my life. It was as if I was transported to another universe ■

Aga Khan Higher Secondary School (AKHSS), Gilgit

Higher Secondary School Certificate • Sep 2005 — May 2010

AKHSS was the true begining of my educational journey. It was during my time at AKHSS that I first began to grasp the immense value of education and its potential to shape a person's life. Aga Khan Higher Secondary School, we are proud of thee!
