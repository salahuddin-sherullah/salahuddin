Book Review - Keynes Hayek: The Clash That Defined Modern Economics

The Clash That Defined Modern Economics by Wapshott is an account of one of the most important debates in modern economics. The Great Depression of the 1930s triggered a crucial discussion among economists to explain the roots and remedies of the business cycle. On the forefront of this economic debate were John Maynard Keynes and Friedrich August von Hayek.
